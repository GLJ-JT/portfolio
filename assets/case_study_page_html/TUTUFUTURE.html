TUTU FUTURE

a strategic rebrand for a Chinese study tour operator expanding beyond hiking trips into academic and cultural programs.

00

problem

TUTU FUTURE, a study tour operator specializing in short-term European programs for Chinese students, had outgrown its original brand identity. The existing logo and visual system were built around a student hiking theme with green color palette—appropriate for their early adventure-focused tours but increasingly misaligned as they expanded into academic courses, art programs, STEM workshops, and language immersion experiences. The hiking-centric brand failed to communicate the breadth and sophistication of their evolving offerings, limiting their appeal to prospective students seeking diverse educational experiences in Europe.

solution

A comprehensive brand refresh that repositioned TUTU FUTURE from an outdoor adventure operator to a multifaceted educational travel platform. The new visual identity system included a modernized logo that balanced academic credibility with youthful energy, an expanded color palette that could flex across different program types (art, STEM, language, culture), and a complete suite of marketing materials optimized for social media campaigns. A custom mascot design added approachability and personality, while the new tone of voice guidelines ensured consistent communication across all touchpoints. The scalable system empowered the team to create cohesive materials for any program type without redesigning from scratch.

TUTU FUTURE's challenge was one of evolution. They had built a loyal following among Chinese students seeking European adventure, but their ambitions had grown beyond hiking trails. As they added academic programs, art workshops, STEM courses, and language immersion experiences to their portfolio, their green-and-outdoorsy brand identity began to feel like a limitation rather than an asset. Prospective students interested in studying Renaissance art in Florence or learning German in Berlin couldn't see themselves in a brand that screamed "backpacking trip."

The rebrand began with strategic positioning. Rather than abandoning their adventurous roots entirely, the goal was to elevate the concept of exploration—from physical journeys to intellectual and cultural discovery. The new logo retained a sense of movement and energy but shed the literal hiking references. Clean, modern typography conveyed academic credibility, while dynamic shapes suggested possibility and growth. The bilingual lockup (TUTU FUTURE 徒途学旅) honored their Chinese student audience while signaling their international scope.

Color became a critical tool for differentiation. The old single-green palette was replaced with a flexible system: vibrant blues for language programs, warm terracottas for art and culture, energetic purples for STEM, and yes, a refined green for outdoor experiences. Each program type could have its own visual personality while remaining unmistakably TUTU FUTURE. The mascot—a friendly, curious character—added warmth and approachability, giving the brand a face that could appear across social media, brochures, and promotional materials without feeling corporate or distant.

The impact was immediate. The new brand system gave TUTU FUTURE the visual language to market their full range of programs with confidence. Social media campaigns could now speak to art students, language learners, and STEM enthusiasts with equal authenticity. Printed brochures and digital materials maintained a cohesive look while celebrating the diversity of experiences on offer. The brand had grown up without losing its spirit, ready to guide the next generation of Chinese students on journeys that would shape their futures.
